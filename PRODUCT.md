# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Retail investors researching before placing trades. They open the dashboard with a specific stock in mind and want a clear, trustworthy AI-powered signal — BUY, SELL, or HOLD — with enough reasoning to feel confident in the output. They are not professional quants; they understand basic trading concepts and need the AI's conclusions presented without jargon overload.

## Product Purpose

An AI-powered stock analysis dashboard that combines real-time market data, technical indicators (RSI, Moving Averages), and a RAG pipeline backed by DeepSeek LLM and FinBERT sentiment analysis to generate explainable trading signals. The product succeeds when a retail investor can open it, select a ticker, run an analysis, and walk away with a clear, reasoned position — all in under two minutes.

## Brand Personality

Modern. Elegant. Data-driven.

Voice is composed and direct. It states conclusions. It does not hype or hedge. The AI surface feels like a co-analyst, not a chatbot or a magic button.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS dark dashboard**: No purple gradients, card-grid hero metrics, or decorative glow orbs. That pattern reads as marketing, not tool.
- **Robinhood / retail app**: No playfulness, gamification, or simplified candy UI. The product handles financial decisions; it earns trust through precision, not charm.
- **Bloomberg Terminal**: No information overload. The AI's job is to synthesize, not to dump every data point on screen.
- **Crypto / NFT aesthetic**: No neon glows, heavy glassmorphism, or speculative energy. The product needs to feel stable and credible.

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence speaks first** — Data and signals lead every surface. Decoration is earned, not added by default.
2. **Clarity over density** — Each panel communicates one primary idea. Cognitive load is a cost; reduce it deliberately.
3. **Trust through restraint** — The AI's conclusions are presented with calm authority. No anxiety-inducing overstatement, no understatement through vagueness.
4. **Every state has a voice** — Loading, error, and empty states are first-class UI moments, not afterthoughts. The product is always communicating.
5. **Signal in three seconds** — A returning user should grasp the active ticker's signal and direction within three seconds of the dashboard rendering.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as baseline: 4.5:1 minimum contrast for body text, keyboard navigable, screen reader compatible. Buy/sell signals cannot rely on color alone — they must carry a text label or icon to remain legible for color-blind users.
